Showcasing the best talent the local community has to offer, our programme provides something for everyone.

Located close to Nottingham and Derby, our aim is to facilitate the highest quality entertainment and promote community activities.

Totally organised and run by volunteers, the Centre, which is a registered charity, is Long Eaton’s premier live arts and entertainment facility.

Tickets are available to purchase at our weekly Saturday Coffee Mornings from 10 till 12 noon & for most shows online via TicketSource.
